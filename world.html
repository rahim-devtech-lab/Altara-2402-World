Altara 2402 — Planet Terra Nova

Planet Terra Nova mirrors Earth in scale and complexity but has its own geography, regions, and corporate infrastructures. Each continent is a learning module, combining 3D visualization with Informatics concepts.

Continents & Learning Modules

Continent Sector Focus Learning Theme Example Scene
Atheris Mining (rare minerals, energy crystals) Database Management – storing, tracking, analyzing mining data 3D mine tunnels with live dashboards showing extraction, inventory, and analytics
Veridia Agriculture & AI irrigation Information Systems Design – smart farming & IoT sensors Smart farm with drones and sensors displaying soil health, water usage, and crop growth
Kryon Logistics & transport ERP & Systems Integration – connecting supply chains 3D logistics hub with real-time cargo tracking, route optimization dashboards
Solara R&D and robotics Human-Computer Interaction – AR/VR interfaces Research campus with holographic UIs, robot prototypes, and AR simulations
Nuvantis HQ City Information Security & Ethics – cybersecurity, access control WolfTech HQ data vault, firewalls, encryption key puzzles, and secure server rooms

Key idea: Each continent is both a visual environment and an interactive learning module, giving users hands-on experience with Informatics principles through immersive storytelling.
